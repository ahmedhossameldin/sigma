Office Spaces Designs

When we build or remodel a new office we understand that its space planning should be impacted by both: our client management and operation thoughts, as well as the architectural design criteria. The main purpose of an office environment is to support its occupants in performing their job efficiently at minimum cost and to the maximum satisfaction.

With different people performing different tasks and activities, we distinguish three different types of office spaces: work spaces, meeting spaces and support spaces. Aligning these types with good circulation confirms the high performance for our clients. We also help the decision maker in how to mix and match between: open space, cubicle offices and team shared space meanwhile considering the remote satellite offices as a new coming concept. Space flexibility, future expansion, warm welcoming receptions, right lighting and smart efficient electro-mechanical systems are all governing concerns in our designs.
