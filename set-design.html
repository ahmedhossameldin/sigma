Studios Set Designs

The studio set design needs to find the perfect balance between looking good and being practical, and needs to represent the overall tone and personality of the show and creating a good impact on the audience. Often working with artistic and creative directors, writers, technicians & producers, our designers are generally good listeners and effective communicators. We possess adequate pre-drawing because many ideas and presentations begin with a sketch or storyboard to be animated later. Our designers used to work under tight deadlines and budgets considering every single detail in the design process.
