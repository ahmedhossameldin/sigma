Mall & Retail Designs

Retail design is one of our creative disciplines, which combines several different areas of expertise together in the design and construction.

For shops and malls, a completely novel aspect of high quality and high sense design is the main stream. Because the primary purpose of retail space is to stock and sell product to consumers, we design the spaces in a way that promotes an enjoyable shopping experience for the consumer and optimum productive for our client. When designing a mall or shop we analyze the floor plan to create circulation which then controls the direction of the traffic flow based on the studied psychology of expected consumer movement pattern within the space.

Straight plan, Pathway plan, Diagonal plan, Curved or Geometric plan every choice has its pros and cons but they are all have a chance.
