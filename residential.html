Residential Facilities Designs

Design of a house is an art form in itself, combining a client’s personal wishes and budget with functionality while lifting the spirit of both the visitors and the regular inhabitants. In contrast to public buildings, a client is much more involved in the house he commissions where we as architects are expected to respect their requirements and take into consideration their perceived status and sensibilities to far larger extent.

The lexical meaning of housing is a building for human habitation that protects them from natural and social harms, being faithful to the basics is always our key in designing or building a new house. The word “habitation” can be explained and interpreted by the architect to a wide range of creative ideas.

When well executed & constructed, the design of a residence can be timeless as it can be seen in the work of “old masters”, based on that we consider each house we build is unique.
